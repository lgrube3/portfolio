Asortly is an Applicant Tracking System (ATS) web application that ranks applicants on culture fit match. An employer creates a job post and culture fit survey. Candidates apply to the job with their resume and culture fit survey responses. The candidate is then matched with the employer from 0% to 100% similar to the OkCupid matching algorithm.

In addition to the culture fit match, employers could view the applicants cover letter, references, past experience, average job duration, and then choose to “like” or “dislike” a candidate.

I was responsible for the UX, Interface Design, and Front-End Development (AngularJS interacting with RESTful service).
